Top 5 Exciting Sports

Football is one of the most popular sports in the world.

It involves two teams trying to score goals using a ball.

Famous competitions include the World Cup and Champions League.

Basketball is played with two teams of five players each.

The aim is to shoot the ball through the opponent's hoop.

It’s a fast-paced game that requires great coordination.

Tennis can be played in singles or doubles formats.

Players use rackets to hit a ball over a net.

Grand Slam tournaments are the highlights of the sport.

Cricket is a bat-and-ball game played between two teams.

It’s very popular in countries like India, England, and Australia.

It includes formats like Test, One Day, and T20 matches.

Swimming is a sport and a survival skill.

It has various styles like freestyle, backstroke, and butterfly.

Olympics feature competitive swimming as a major event.
